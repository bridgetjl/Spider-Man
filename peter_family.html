Family

Family:

 An important priority of Peter’s is protecting his family because he doesn’t have much left. Unfortunately, his parents died when he was very little and his Uncle Ben was killed by a robber.

Aunt May:

Peter’s Aunt May is a central character throughout the serious and is often in danger due to Peter’s superhero pursuits. He hides his identity from her but she eventually finds out and supports his good work. Spider-Man’s enemies in the past have used her to get to him by threatening her life and even at one point forcing Peter to erase his marriage to Mary Jane in order to protect his Aunt.

Uncle Ben:

Peter’s uncle is a huge inspiration to him and his death is the reason he began to use his powers for good.

Richard and Mary Parker:

Peter Parker’s parents who died when he was very young and left him in the care of his aunt and uncle.
